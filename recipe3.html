Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g shrimp or chicken (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, crushed
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Procedure

  1. Cook the noodles: Follow package instructions to cook the rice noodles. Drain and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry: Heat oil in a pan, scramble the eggs, then add garlic and your choice of shrimp or chicken. Cook until done.
  3. Make the sauce: In a small bowl, mix fish sauce, tamarind paste, sugar, and chili flakes.
  4. Combine: Add cooked noodles to the pan, pour in the sauce, and toss everything together.
  5. Serve: Top with crushed peanuts, chopped spring onions, and a squeeze of lime.
  6. Enjoy! Your quick and tasty Pad Thai is ready!
