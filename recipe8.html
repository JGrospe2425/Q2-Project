Quick Ramen

Ingredients

  • 1 pack instant ramen noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked chicken or tofu (optional)
  • 1/2 cup spinach or bok choy
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • Chili flakes or sriracha (optional)

Procedure

  1. Boil Water: Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Cook Noodles: Add the ramen noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  3. Add Egg: Crack an egg into the pot and let it cook for 1-2 minutes, or until desired doneness.
  4. Add Flavors: Stir in the seasoning packet, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  5. Add Veggies: Add spinach or bok choy and cook for another minute until wilted.
  6. Serve: Top with green onions, chicken or tofu (if using), and a sprinkle of chili flakes or sriracha for heat.
