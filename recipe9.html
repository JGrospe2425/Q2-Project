Mango Float

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • 2 cups all-purpose cream
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1 pack graham crackers
  • Graham cracker crumbs (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Cream Mixture: In a bowl, mix the all-purpose cream and condensed milk until well combined.
  2. Layer the Dessert: In a rectangular dish, arrange a layer of graham crackers at the bottom. Spread a layer of the cream mixture over the crackers. Add a layer of sliced mangoes on top.
  3. Repeat Layers: Continue layering the graham crackers, cream mixture, and mango slices until all ingredients are used, finishing with the cream mixture.
  4. Top and Chill: Sprinkle graham cracker crumbs on the top layer. Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until set.
  5. Serve: Slice and serve chilled.
  6. Enjoy! Your delicious mango float is ready to enjoy!
