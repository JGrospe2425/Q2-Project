Lumpia

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork or chicken
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup sliced green beans
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Procedure

  1. Cook the filling: In a pan, saut√© onions, garlic, and ground meat until cooked. Add the carrots, green beans, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  2. Wrap the lumpia: Place a spoonful of the filling on a lumpia wrapper, fold in the sides, and roll tightly.
  3. Fry: Heat oil in a pan. Fry lumpia until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Serve: Drain on paper towels and serve with a dipping sauce like sweet and sour sauce or vinegar.
  5. Enjoy your crispy Lumpia!
