Kimbap

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • 1/2 cucumber, julienned
  • 1/2 carrot, julienned
  • 2 eggs, scrambled and cooked into a thin omelette
  • 1/2 cup cooked beef or fish cakes (optional)

Procedure

  1. Prepare Rice: Mix the cooked rice with sesame oil, sugar, and salt.
  2. Prepare Fillings: Cut the cucumber, carrot, and omelette into strips.
  3. Assemble Kimbap: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat, spread a thin layer of rice, and add the fillings.
  4. Roll: Roll tightly from one end to the other.
  5. Slice: Cut the rolled kimbap into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Enjoy: Your fresh Kimbap is ready to enjoy!
