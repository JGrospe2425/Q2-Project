Bánh Flan

Ingredients

  • Caramel: 1 cup sugar, 1/4 cup water
  • Custard: 1 can condensed milk, 1 can evaporated milk, 5 eggs, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 tsp vanilla

Procedure

  1. Make caramel: Combine sugar and water in a pan, cook over medium heat until golden. Pour into ramekins and let it harden.
  2. Make custard: Whisk all custard ingredients until smooth. Strain to remove bubbles.
  3. Bake: Pour custard over caramel. Place ramekins in a water bath. Bake at 325°F (160°C) for 45-60 minutes, until set.
  4. Cool & Serve: Let cool, refrigerate for 2 hours, then invert onto a plate.
  5. Enjoy your Bánh Flan!
