Spring Rolls

Ingredients for Spring Rolls

  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 1 cup cooked rice noodles
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, julienned
  • 1/2 cup cooked shrimp or tofu, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Lettuce leaves

Ingredients for Peanut Sauce

  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Water (to thin)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Arrange all the fillings for easy assembly.
  2. Soften Rice Paper: Dip one rice paper wrapper in warm water for about 10 seconds until soft. Place on a damp towel.
  3. Assemble Rolls: Lay lettuce, rice noodles, carrots, cucumber, shrimp or tofu, and herbs in the center of the wrapper. Fold the bottom over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll tightly.
  4. Make Peanut Sauce: In a small bowl, mix peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, and water until smooth.
  5. Serve: Serve the spring rolls with peanut sauce for dipping.
  6. Enjoy! Your fresh and flavorful spring rolls are ready!
